Our Story

Built on the
craft, not the trend.

Fade & Co. opened in Osu in 2013 with two chairs and one straight razor. Today it's six chairs strong — and the standard hasn't moved an inch.

Since 2013

From a rented back room to Accra's chair of choice.

Our founder learned to cut from his uncle's barbershop in Kumasi, then spent three years training in straight-razor technique before opening the first Fade & Co. chair. The shop grew the same way every good cut does — one client, one referral, one Saturday morning at a time.

We still run it the way we started: appointments treated like appointments, tools kept sharp and clean, and a barber who actually looks at your hairline before touching a clipper to it.

How we work

Three things we don't compromise on, no matter how busy the floor gets.

On time

Your slot is your slot

Booked appointments are seen within five minutes of the time you chose. No stacking, no overbooking.

Clean tools

Sanitised, every client

Clippers, combs, and razors are cleaned and sanitised between every single chair session.

Real training

No apprentice on your hairline

Every barber here completed a supervised apprenticeship before working solo on clients.
